How It Works?

Digital Flow of Biodegradable Waste

BioBinConnect manages every step from household waste pickup to feed supply and compost production inside a single platform.

Request & Pickup

Households log in, schedule biodegradable waste pickup, and receive confirmations and alerts online.

Collector Routing

Collectors follow assigned routes, collect segregated waste, and update pickup status in real time.

Feed & Compost Processing

Collected waste is sorted so usable portions go to farmers as feed and the rest to compost bins.

Compost Listing & Delivery

Ready compost batches are listed in the system, and farmers place orders and track their deliveries.
