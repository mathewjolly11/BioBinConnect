COLLECTOR USER GUIDE

WHO ARE COLLECTORS?
Licensed professionals who collect waste from households and deliver to farmers/managers.

HOW TO REGISTER:
1. Visit website, click "Sign Up"
2. Select "Collector" role
3. Fill form: Name, License Number, Vehicle Number, Phone, Email, Password
4. Upload license photo
5. Wait for admin approval

HOW TO LOG COLLECTION:
1. Visit household and collect waste
2. Login to dashboard
3. Go to "Log Collection"
4. Enter actual weight collected
5. Add notes (optional)
6. Submit
7. Email sent to household automatically

EARNINGS:
- Rate: ₹1,000 per day worked
- Example: Work 5 days = ₹5,000
- Payment: Processed by admin
- Track unpaid vs paid earnings in dashboard

FEATURES:
- View assigned pickups for today
- Log collections
- View inventory (Available/Sold/Expired)
- Sales orders from farmers
- Update delivery status
- Earnings tracker

INVENTORY:
- Waste expires after 30 days automatically
- Status: Available → Sold → Expired
- Farmers can order your available waste

COMMON QUESTIONS:
Q: How do I get assigned pickups?
A: Admin assigns you to routes and days. Auto-assigned when households request.

Q: When do I get paid?
A: Admin processes payments. You'll get email confirmation.

Q: What happens after 30 days?
A: Waste auto-expires and becomes unavailable.
