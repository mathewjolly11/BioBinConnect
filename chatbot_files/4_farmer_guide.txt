FARMER USER GUIDE

WHO ARE FARMERS?
Agricultural users who purchase organic waste and compost for farming.

HOW TO REGISTER:
1. Visit website, click "Sign Up"
2. Select "Farmer" role
3. Fill form: Name, Aadhaar, Farm Name, Land Size, Phone, Email, Password
4. Upload Aadhaar photo
5. Wait for admin approval

PRODUCTS AVAILABLE:

A. ORGANIC WASTE:
   - Raw biodegradable waste from households
   - Price: ₹10 per kg
   - Use: Direct fertilizer, soil amendment
   - Delivered by collectors

B. COMPOST:
   - Processed organic fertilizer
   - Quality grades:
     * Premium: ₹250-300/kg (highest quality)
     * Grade A: ₹200-250/kg
     * Grade B: ₹150-200/kg
     * Grade C: ₹100-150/kg
   - Average: ₹200/kg
   - Delivered by managers

HOW TO ORDER:
1. Login to dashboard
2. Browse "Waste" or "Compost"
3. Select product
4. Enter quantity needed
5. Enter delivery address
6. Select payment method (UPI/Cash/Card)
7. Review total amount
8. Place order
9. Wait for admin to assign delivery
10. Receive product
11. Make payment

FEATURES:
- Browse waste inventory
- Browse compost batches
- Place orders
- View order history
- Track order status
- Payment processing

ORDER STATUS:
- Pending: Order placed
- Processing: Admin assigned delivery
- Shipped: Out for delivery
- Delivered: Product received
- Completed: Payment done

COMMON QUESTIONS:
Q: What's difference between waste and compost?
A: Waste is raw material. Compost is processed, high-quality fertilizer.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Typically 1-3 days after admin assigns.

Q: Can I return products?
A: Contact admin for quality issues.
