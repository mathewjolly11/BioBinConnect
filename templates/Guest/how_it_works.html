How It Works

A simple 4-step process to sustainable waste management

1

Sign Up

Create your account by selecting your role and providing necessary details. Wait for admin approval.

2

Schedule Pickup

Choose your bin type, select a convenient date, and complete the payment securely online.

3

Collection

Our assigned collector picks up your biodegradable waste on the scheduled date and time.

4

Composting

Waste is converted into nutrient-rich compost and sold to farmers for sustainable agriculture.
