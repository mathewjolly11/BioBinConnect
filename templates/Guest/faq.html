Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about BioBinConnect

General Questions

What is BioBinConnect?

BioBinConnect is a web-based platform that connects households, waste collectors, compost managers, and farmers to create a sustainable circular economy for biodegradable waste management.

Who can use BioBinConnect?

BioBinConnect serves four types of users: Households (waste generators), Collectors (pickup service providers), Compost Managers (waste processors), and Farmers (compost buyers).

How does the circular economy work?

Households generate biodegradable waste → Collectors pick it up → Compost Managers convert it into nutrient-rich compost → Farmers purchase the compost for sustainable agriculture. This creates a zero-waste cycle.

For Households

How do I schedule a pickup?

After registering and logging in, navigate to "Request Pickup" from your dashboard. Select your bin type, choose a date, and complete the payment. A collector will be automatically assigned based on your location.

What types of waste can I dispose of?

BioBinConnect accepts biodegradable waste including food scraps, vegetable peels, fruit waste, garden waste, and other organic materials. We offer different bin sizes to suit your needs.

How do I track my pickup request?

Go to "My Requests" in your dashboard to view all your pickup requests, their status (Pending, Approved, Completed), assigned collector information, and scheduled dates.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept UPI and Cash on Delivery (COD). Payment is required when scheduling a pickup request.

Account & Technical

How do I create an account?

Click "Sign Up" or "Login/Register" in the navigation menu. Fill in your details, select your role (Household, Collector, Compost Manager, or Farmer), and submit. Admin approval is required before you can log in.

Why is my account not verified?

All new accounts require admin verification to ensure authenticity and maintain platform quality. You'll receive confirmation once your account is approved.

Can I update my profile information?

Yes! Go to "My Profile" from your dashboard and click "Edit Profile" to update your personal information, contact details, and address.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account from the "Edit Profile" page. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.
