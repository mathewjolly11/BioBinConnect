                          PLATFORM OVERVIEW
================================================================================

WHAT IS BIOBINCONNECT?
BioBinConnect is a comprehensive web-based biodegradable waste management platform that connects households, waste collectors, compost managers, and farmers in a circular economy ecosystem. The platform digitizes and automates the entire waste management lifecycle from collection to composting to agricultural use.

MISSION:
To create a sustainable, zero-waste circular economy by connecting waste generators with processors and end-users through technology.

TECHNOLOGY STACK:
- Framework: Django 6.0 (Python)
- Database: MySQL 8.0
- Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (Vanilla)
- Email: SMTP (Gmail)
- Operating System: Cross-platform (Windows, Linux, macOS)

Compost managers are licensed facilities that convert organic waste into high-quality compost through controlled decomposition processes.

  * Weight available (kg)
  * Collection date
  * Status

BATCH MANAGEMENT:

CREATE COMPOST BATCH:

Step 1: Select source waste
   - Choose from available waste inventory
   - Can select multiple waste items
   - Total source waste calculated

Step 2: Enter batch name
   - Unique identifier for batch
   - Example: "Batch AA", "Batch BB"

Step 3: System auto-calculates compost output
   - Conversion ratio: 4:1
   - Formula: Source Waste ÷ 4 = Compost Output
   - Example: 160kg waste → 40kg compost

Step 4: Select quality grade
   - Premium: Highest quality
   - Grade A: High quality
   - Grade B: Medium quality
   - Grade C: Standard quality

Step 5: Set price per kg
   - Based on grade selected
   - Suggested ranges:
     * Premium: ₹250-300/kg
     * Grade A: ₹200-250/kg
     * Grade B: ₹150-200/kg
     * Grade C: ₹100-150/kg

Step 6: Create batch
   - Initial status: Processing
   - Batch ID generated
   - Source waste marked as used

MANAGE BATCHES:
- View all batches
- Filter by status:
  * Processing: Currently composting
  * Ready: Available for sale
  * Sold: Purchased by farmers
- Batch details displayed:
  * Batch name
  * Source waste amount
  * Compost produced
  * Quality grade
  * Price per kg
  * Stock remaining
  * Creation date
  * Status

EDIT BATCH:
- Modify quality grade
- Update price per kg
- Change batch status
- Update batch information

UPDATE STATUS:
Status Flow:
1. Processing → Composting in progress
2. Ready → Composting complete, available for sale
3. Sold → Automatically updated when farmer orders

CONVERSION SYSTEM:

DEFAULT RATIO: 4:1
- 4 kg of waste produces 1 kg of compost
- Configurable by admin
- Examples:
  * 160kg waste → 40kg compost
  * 200kg waste → 50kg compost
  * 80kg waste → 20kg compost

QUALITY GRADING & PRICING:

GRADING CRITERIA:
- Premium: Perfect decomposition, rich nutrients, fine texture
- Grade A: Excellent quality, good nutrients
- Grade B: Good quality, adequate nutrients
- Grade C: Standard quality, basic nutrients

PRICING GUIDELINES:

7. Farmer receives product
8. Farmer makes payment
9. Admin pays collector salary

                          FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
================================================================================

GENERAL QUESTIONS:

Q: What is BioBinConnect?
A: BioBinConnect is a web-based platform that connects households, waste collectors, compost managers, and farmers to create a circular economy for biodegradable waste management.

Q: Who can use BioBinConnect?
A: Five types of users: Households (waste generators), Collectors (pickup agents), Compost Managers (waste processors), Farmers (end consumers), and Admin (system manager).

Q: How do I register?
A: Visit the website, click "Sign Up", select your role, fill the registration form, upload required documents, and wait for admin approval.

Q: How long does approval take?
A: Admin typically reviews and approves registrations within 24-48 hours.

Q: Is BioBinConnect free to use?
A: Households pay monthly service fees (₹50-₹100). Other stakeholders earn money through the platform.

HOUSEHOLD QUESTIONS:

Q: How do I request a pickup?
A: Login, go to "Request Pickup", select bin type, enter estimated weight, and submit. A collector will be auto-assigned.

Q: How much does pickup service cost?
A: Medium bin (25kg): ₹50/month, Large bin (50kg): ₹100/month.

Q: When will my waste be collected?
A: Based on your route's schedule. You'll receive an email with pickup details and assigned collector.

Q: Can I choose my collector?
A: No, collectors are automatically assigned based on your route and the current day.

Q: How do I pay?
A: Go to "Make Payment", select payment method (UPI/Cash/Card), and complete the transaction.

COLLECTOR QUESTIONS:

Q: How do I get assigned to pickups?
A: Admin assigns you to routes and days. When households on your route request pickups on your assigned days, you're automatically assigned.

Q: How much do I earn?
A: ₹1,000 per day worked. Days are counted when you log collections.

Q: How do I log a collection?
A: After collecting waste, go to "Log Collection", enter actual weight, add notes, and submit.

Q: When do I get paid?
A: Admin processes salary payments. You'll receive email confirmation when paid.

Q: What happens to waste after 30 days?
A: Waste automatically expires and becomes unavailable for sale.

FARMER QUESTIONS:

Q: What products can I buy?
A: Two types: Organic waste (₹10/kg) and Compost (₹100-300/kg based on grade).

Q: What's the difference between waste and compost?
A: Waste is raw biodegradable material. Compost is processed, high-quality organic fertilizer.

Q: How do I place an order?
A: Browse products, select quantity, enter delivery address, choose payment method, and place order.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Admin assigns delivery after order placement. Typical delivery: 1-3 days.

Q: Can I return products?
A: Contact admin for any quality issues or concerns.

COMPOST MANAGER QUESTIONS:

Q: How do I create a batch?
A: Select available waste, enter batch name, system calculates output, select grade, set price, and create.

Q: What is the conversion ratio?
A: 4:1 ratio - 4kg of waste produces 1kg of compost.

Q: How do I price my compost?
A: Based on quality grade: Premium (₹250-300), A (₹200-250), B (₹150-200), C (₹100-150).

Q: How much do I earn?
A: ₹1,000 per day worked. Days are counted when you create batches.

Q: When do I get paid?
A: Admin processes salary payments. You'll receive email confirmation.

ADMIN QUESTIONS:

Q: How do I approve users?
A: Go to "User Management", review pending registrations, verify documents, and approve or reject.

Q: How do I assign collectors to routes?
A: Go to "Collector Assignment", select collector, route, and day of week.

Q: How do I process salaries?
A: Go to "Salary Management", view unpaid days, select days to pay, enter UPI PIN, and confirm.

Q: How do I view reports?
A: Go to "Reports" or "Payment Analytics" for comprehensive financial and operational reports.

Q: Can I export data?
A: Yes, most reports have Excel and PDF export options.

TECHNICAL QUESTIONS:

A: Django 6.0 (Python), MySQL 8.0, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript.

Q: Is it mobile-friendly?
A: Yes, responsive design works on all devices.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes, we use CSRF protection, password hashing, and secure authentication.

Q: Can I access offline?
A: No, internet connection required. Mobile app with offline features is planned.

Q: What browsers are supported?
A: All modern browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge.
