PRICING & PAYMENTS

HOUSEHOLD COSTS:
- Medium Bin (25kg): ₹50 per month
- Large Bin (50kg): ₹100 per month
- Payment methods: UPI, Cash, Card

FARMER PURCHASES:
A. Organic Waste:
   - Price: ₹10 per kg
   - From collectors

B. Compost:
   - Premium: ₹250-300/kg
   - Grade A: ₹200-250/kg
   - Grade B: ₹150-200/kg
   - Grade C: ₹100-150/kg
   - Average: ₹200/kg
   - From compost managers

COLLECTOR EARNINGS:
- Rate: ₹1,000 per day worked
- Example: 5 days = ₹5,000
- Paid by admin

MANAGER EARNINGS:
- Rate: ₹1,000 per day worked
- Example: 5 days = ₹5,000
- Paid by admin

PAYMENT METHODS:
All users can use:
- UPI (Unified Payments Interface)
- Cash on Delivery
- Card Payment

TRANSACTION TRACKING:
Every payment includes:
- Transaction ID (auto-generated)
- Amount
- Payment method
- Date and time
- Status

PAYMENT SECURITY:
- Salary payments require UPI PIN
- All transactions recorded
- Email confirmations sent
- Complete audit trail
