HOUSEHOLD USER GUIDE

WHO ARE HOUSEHOLDS?
Residential users who generate organic waste and need regular pickup services.

HOW TO REGISTER:
1. Visit website, click "Sign Up"
2. Select "Household" role
3. Fill form: Name, Aadhaar, House Number, Phone, Email, Password
4. Upload photo
5. Wait for admin approval (24-48 hours)

HOW TO REQUEST PICKUP:
1. Login to dashboard
2. Click "Request Pickup"
3. Select bin type:
   - Medium (25kg): ₹50/month
   - Large (50kg): ₹100/month
4. Enter estimated weight
5. Submit - Collector auto-assigned
6. Receive email confirmation

PAYMENT:
- Monthly service fee: ₹50 or ₹100
- Payment methods: UPI, Cash, Card
- Go to "Make Payment" to pay

FEATURES:
- Dashboard with statistics
- Request pickup
- View pickup history
- Payment history
- Profile management
- Email notifications

COMMON QUESTIONS:
Q: When will waste be collected?
A: Based on your route schedule. You'll get email with details.

Q: Can I choose my collector?
A: No, auto-assigned based on route and day.

Q: How do I pay?
A: Go to "Make Payment", select method, complete transaction.
